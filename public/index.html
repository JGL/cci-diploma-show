Welcome to the CCI Diploma Show 2020

Since we can no longer visit CCI physically, we decided to bring CCI to you! Feel free to wander around our virtual campus and get to know more about the projects we have been working on over the past few months. Here, you can eat a pizza, merge your face with someone else's, blow some bubbles, touch a stranger through your laptop, or help a starfish return home. Use the chat interface to talk to other attendees!

This show was built collaboratively by students from the first cohort of the CCI Diploma in Creative Computing, based on LIKELIKE Online.
